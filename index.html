Founded in 2015, Velour began with a simple vision: to create timeless wardrobe essentials that transcend seasonal trends. Our philosophy is rooted in sustainable luxury—crafting pieces that are both beautiful and responsible.

Every garment in our collection is designed to last, using premium materials and artisanal techniques that honor traditional craftsmanship while embracing modern innovation.
