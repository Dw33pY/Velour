Our Story

Crafting timeless essentials with purpose and passion since 2015

Our Mission

At Velour, we believe in creating clothing that lasts—both in quality and style. Our mission is to provide timeless wardrobe essentials that reduce waste and promote conscious consumption.

Every piece is designed with intention, crafted with care, and made to be worn for years to come. We're committed to sustainable practices, ethical production, and exceptional quality.

- Aristotle

Our Journey

2015

Velour is Born

Founded in a small Brooklyn studio with a vision for sustainable luxury fashion and a collection of 12 essential pieces.

2017

First Flagship Store

Opened our first physical location in SoHo, New York, focusing on creating an immersive brand experience.

2020

Carbon Neutral Initiative

Became the first fashion brand in our category to achieve 100% carbon neutrality across our supply chain.

2023

Global Expansion

Launched international shipping to 50+ countries and opened flagship stores in London and Tokyo.

2026

Looking Ahead

Continuing to innovate in sustainable materials while maintaining our commitment to timeless design and ethical production.
